# Product Overview

A simple 3-tier e-commerce web application that allows users to browse products, view product details with reviews, and manage a shopping cart.

## Key Features

- Product catalog with 20 items (using emojis as product images)
- Product detail pages with descriptions, pricing, and customer reviews
- Shopping cart functionality (add, update quantity, remove items)
- Responsive UI design

## User Flow

1. Browse products on home page
2. Click product to view details and reviews
3. Add items to cart with quantity selection
4. Manage cart (update quantities, remove items)
